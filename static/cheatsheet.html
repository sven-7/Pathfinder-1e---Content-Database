Combat Maneuvers
CMB = BAB + Str mod + size mod  ·  CMD = 10 + BAB + Str + Dex + size
ManeuverAoOSuccess Effect
Bull Rush Yes* Push foe back 5 ft; +5 ft per 5 over CMD. You may follow.
Dirty Trick Yes* Apply blinded/entangled/etc. 1 rnd; +1 rnd per 5 over CMD. Remove = move action.
Disarm Yes* Foe drops weapon. If 10+ over CMD, you grab it. Defender may try to disarm you.
Drag Yes* Pull foe 5 ft toward you; +5 ft per 5 over CMD. You may follow.
Grapple Yes* Grappled. Each round: maintain, move, damage, pin, or tie up. Caster: concentration DC 10 + CMB.
Overrun Yes* Move through foe's space. Foe chooses avoid or block (block = CMB contest).
Reposition Yes* Move foe to any adjacent square; +5 ft per 5 over CMD.
Steal Yes* Take one visible item from foe. Must have a free hand.
Sunder Yes* Deal damage to held/worn item (bypasses hardness if 5+ over CMD).
Trip Yes* Foe knocked prone. If 10+ over CMD, also disarm. Defender may try to trip you.
* No AoO if you have the corresponding Improved feat (Improved Bull Rush, etc.).

Conditions
Blinded
−2 AC, lose Dex bonus; −2 attacks; 50% miss chance; half speed
Broken
Item: −2 attack/damage; magic items function at half strength or not at all
Confused
d%: 1–25 act normally; 26–50 do nothing; 51–75 attack self; 76–100 attack nearest
Cowering
Lose Dex bonus; −2 AC; no actions; more severe than shaken
Dazed
No actions (including AoOs); AC unaffected; 1 round only
Dazzled
−1 attack rolls and Perception checks
Deafened
−4 initiative; −4 Perception (sound); 20% arcane spell failure
Disabled
0 or 1 HP; only standard or move action; strenuous activity = 1 damage/action
Dying
−1 to −Con HP; unconscious; DC 10 Con to stabilize each round or −1 HP
Energy Drained
−1 per negative level to attacks, saves, checks, spell DCs, max HP
Entangled
−2 attack; −4 Dex; concentration DC 15+SL to cast; may be rooted
Exhausted
Half speed; −6 Str and Dex; rest 1 hr → fatigued. Strenuous = 1 hr to exhaust again
Fatigued
−2 Str and Dex; can't run or charge; 8 hrs rest or remove fatigue to recover
Flat-Footed
Lose Dex bonus to AC; can't make AoO (unless Combat Reflexes)
Frightened
−2 attacks/saves/checks; must flee if possible; drop held items if can't flee
Grappled
−4 Dex; −2 attack and combat maneuvers; no move; concentration DC 10+CMB
Helpless
Paralyzed/held/bound; effective Dex 0; subject to coup de grace
Incorporeal
Immune to non-magic attacks; 50% chance to ignore magic; no Str score
Invisible
+2 attacks; opponents lose Dex to AC; can't be seen; Stealth check to hide location
Nauseated
Only move actions; no attacks, spells, or abilities requiring concentration
Panicked
Drop items, flee; −2 saves; 50% won't attack; more severe than frightened
Paralyzed
Effective Str and Dex 0; helpless; can only take purely mental actions
Petrified
Turned to stone; unconscious; DR 8/— while stone; breaking off parts = permanent
Pinned
Helpless; more severe grappled; −4 AC, lose Dex; concentration DC 10+CMB+4
Prone
−4 melee attacks; +4 AC vs ranged; −4 AC vs melee; stand up = move action (AoO)
Shaken
−2 attacks, saves, skill checks, ability checks
Sickened
−2 attacks, damage, saves, skill checks, ability checks
Staggered
Only a standard or move action (not both); free/swift/immediate still allowed
Stunned
Drop held items; −2 AC; lose Dex; no actions; creatures can still AoO you
Unconscious
Knocked out; helpless; effective Dex and Str 0
Fear: Shaken < Frightened < Panicked < Cowering. Fatigue: Fatigued < Exhausted.
